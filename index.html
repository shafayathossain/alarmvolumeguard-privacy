01

Overview

🔒

AlarmVolumeGuard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. Everything the app needs is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

The app monitors your Android alarm volume and restores it to your chosen level using a foreground service. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising networks.

02

Data We Store

The following values are saved locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. None are transmitted remotely.

Data Why it's needed Remote?
Guard enabled / disabled Restore guard state after reboot No
Guard volume level Know which level to protect No
Subscription status Check whether Pro is unlocked No

We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other personal data.

03

Permissions

Each permission is used only for the stated purpose — nothing else.

🔊

MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS

Reads and restores your alarm stream volume when it drops below your set level.

🔕

ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY

Ensures Do Not Disturb mode cannot silently override your alarm volume.

🔔

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Shows a persistent notification on Android 13+ indicating the guard service is active.

⚡

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Restarts the guard automatically after a device reboot if you had it enabled.

⚙️

FOREGROUND_SERVICE

Runs a persistent background service to watch alarm volume in real time via a ContentObserver.

04

In-App Purchases

AlarmVolumeGuard offers a Pro subscription (alarm_volume_guard_pro_monthly) that unlocks the Volume Guard feature. All transactions are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see, handle, or store your payment information.

💳

The app only receives a yes / no confirmation of whether an active subscription exists. No financial data touches our code.

To cancel your subscription: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

05

Third-Party Services

The only third-party integration is Google Play Billing for subscription payments. It is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

There are no analytics SDKs (Firebase, Crashlytics, Amplitude), no advertising networks, and no social media integrations in this app.

06

Data Retention

All data (guard state, volume level, subscription cache) lives in Android SharedPreferences and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

You can clear it manually at any time: Settings → Apps → AlarmVolumeGuard → Clear Data.

07

Children's Privacy

AlarmVolumeGuard does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app has no registration flows, user accounts, or data collection mechanisms that would be relevant to this concern.
