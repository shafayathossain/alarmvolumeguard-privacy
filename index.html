Privacy Policy

Effective date:

AlarmVolumeGuard is built on a simple principle: your device, your data. This policy explains exactly what information the app uses, why it uses it, and what it never does.

01 Overview

AlarmVolumeGuard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data the app needs to function is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

The app's sole purpose is to monitor and maintain your Android alarm volume at a level you choose, using a foreground service that runs in the background. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.

02 Information We Collect

AlarmVolumeGuard stores only the following data, locally on your device, using Android SharedPreferences:

Data Purpose Stored remotely?
Guard enabled/disabled state Restore guard state after a reboot No
Guard volume level (integer) Know which volume level to protect No
Subscription status (boolean) Determine whether the guard feature is unlocked No

We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash logs, or any other personal data.

03 Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions. Each is used solely for the stated purpose and nothing else.

🔊

MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS

Allows the app to read and restore your alarm stream volume when it drops below your set level.

🔕

ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY

Required to ensure Do Not Disturb mode does not override alarm volume settings.

🔔

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Displays a persistent notification (Android 13+) indicating the guard service is active. Required for foreground services.

⚡

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Automatically restarts the guard after a device reboot if you had previously enabled it.

⚙️

FOREGROUND_SERVICE

Runs a persistent background service to monitor alarm volume in real time using a system ContentObserver.

04 In-App Purchases

AlarmVolumeGuard offers a Pro subscription (guard_pro_monthly) that unlocks the Volume Guard feature. All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment information at any point.

The app only receives a confirmation of whether a valid active subscription exists. No financial data is stored on your device or our servers.

To manage or cancel your subscription, visit Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

05 Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into AlarmVolumeGuard is Google Play Billing, used exclusively to process subscription payments. This service is governed by Google's Privacy Policy .

No analytics SDKs (such as Firebase, Crashlytics, or Amplitude), advertising networks, or social media integrations are included in this app.

06 Data Retention

All data (guard state, volume level, subscription cache) is stored locally on your device inside Android's SharedPreferences. This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. You can also clear it at any time via Settings → Apps → AlarmVolumeGuard → Clear Data.

07 Children's Privacy

AlarmVolumeGuard does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no user accounts, registration flows, or data collection that would be relevant to this concern.
